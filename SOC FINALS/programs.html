Our services

BSIT

BSIT focuses on the application of technology to solve business problems. It covers areas such as software development, networking, database management, cybersecurity, and project management. Graduates are equipped with skills to design, implement, and manage IT systems in various industries.

BSCS

BSCS is a comprehensive program that covers both theoretical concepts and practical applications of computer science. It includes topics such as algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and computer architecture. Graduates are prepared for careers in software development, research, and technology innovation.

BSIS

BSIS focuses on the intersection of business and technology. It covers areas such as information management, systems analysis and design, database administration, and IT strategy. Graduates possess the skills to analyze organizational needs, design information systems solutions, and manage technology resources to support business objectives.
