Who Are We?

Welcome to the School of Computer Studies, where innovation meets education! Whether you're interested in diving into the world of Information Technology, Computer Science, or Information Systems, our programs cater to your passion for technology and its applications. With Bachelor of Science degrees in Information Technology (BSIT), Computer Science (BSCS), and Information Systems (BSIS), we provide a dynamic learning environment where students can explore cutting-edge concepts, develop practical skills, and prepare for rewarding careers in the ever-evolving field of computing. Join us as we embark on a journey to shape the future of technology together!
